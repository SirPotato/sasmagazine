A Dive into Quantum Mechanics

by: Andrew Hojel

Bio

Andrew Hojel was born in Mexico City, Mexico in 2000 and lived there until he was six years old. He then moved to Austin, TX, where he currently resides. His interest in Quantum Mechanics began when a teacher recommended that he read Quantum: A Guide for the Perplexed by Jim Al-Khalili. He instantly fell in love with the incredible laws of the subatomic world, but he was shocked by how difficult it was to find simple, clear explanations about the concepts and theories in Quantum Mechanics. This spurred him to write this article.
